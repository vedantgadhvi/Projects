Vedant Gadhvi

ABOUT

Vedant Gadhvi is a singer/songwriter based in Ahmedabad, singing for more than 8 years now. He marked his debut as a vocalist with the acoustic rendition of Dil-e-beqaraar, his first original. His sound and style of composition stands out from the mainstream sound, making his composition skills a big asset for him. He also possess the ability to pen down his own lyrics. Being an active participant in gigs and shows around the city has given him a considerable experience performing live too.
